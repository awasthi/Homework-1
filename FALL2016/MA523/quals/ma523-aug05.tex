  \item Find a solution of the Cauchy problem
    \[
      \left\{
        \begin{aligned}
          &yu_x+xu_y=xy,\\
          &u=1&&\text{on \(S^1=\bigl\{\,x^2+y^2=1\,\bigr\}\)}.
        \end{aligned}
      \right.
    \]
  \item Is the solution unique in a neighborhood of the point \((1,0)\)?
    Justify your answer.

  \]
