    A\coloneq
    \begin{bmatrix}
      1&1&1\\
      1&2&t\\
      1&4&t^2
    \end{bmatrix}.
  \]
  \begin{parts}
    \part[8] Compute the determinant of $A$ using any method you like.
    \part[4] For what values of $t$ is $A$ nonsingular?
