  \question Let $\ell^2(\bbR)$ be the set of all sequences $\{a_n\}$ whose
  sum of squares converges, i.e.,
  \[
    \sum_{n=1}^\infty {a_n}^2<\infty.
  \]
  Show that $\ell^2(\bbR)$ is a vector space.
